About

My name is Todd Garraway aka "Big Shepard" and moving the clippers/scissors is second nature to me. With a focus mainly on quality haircuts over everything, I create a style for each client that is mesmerizing for them that push the boundaries of the current barber landscape

Over the years, my dedication to these haircuts has garnered me numerous compliments showcasing my mastery and innovative approach.

From the bustling streets of Hollywood to the sun-soaked shores of Playa Del Rey and the vibrant city of Los Angeles, I have traveled far and wide to London, Chicago, New York, and Miami even to the beautiful country of Columbia with these clippers leaving an incredible mark with my work and personality. Join me in this mobile barber truck as we discover where haircuts meets the mind. Now join me in this mobile barber truck where haircuts meets the mind. This is Progress Over Poverty.

Faq

As a mobile barbershop, we often receive inquiries from clients regarding availability to process booking of an appointment To help address these common queries, I have compiled a list of frequently asked questions. If you have any additional inquiries, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.

What services are offered?

We offer a full range of barbershop services including haircuts, beard trims, shaves, and styling. Our team is equipped to handle various hair types and styles, ensuring that you get a personalized and professional service every time. Visit the pricing page to find out more!

What should I do before the visit?

Browse through hairstyles online or consider what has worked for you in the past. Feel free to save pictures to show to your barber as a reference.

Are the tools hygienic and safe?

Absolutely! We adhere to strict hygiene protocols. All our barbers are trained in sanitation and disinfection practices. Tools are sterilized before and after each use, and we use single-use items where necessary. Your health and safety, as well as that of our staff, are our top priority.
